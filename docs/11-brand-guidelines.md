## UX Principles

### Drunk-Friendly Design
- Minimum touch target: **48×48px** on all interactive elements
- Maximum **3 taps** to reach any core action
- Large, high-contrast text — readable in low light
- Avoid small icons without labels on primary actions
- Confirm dialogs for destructive actions only — keep flow fast

### Dark Theme Always
- Every screen: `#0D0D0F` background
- No light mode — this is a nightlife app
- Neon purple highlights guide the eye to primary actions

### Motion & Feedback
- Check-in success: satisfying animated confirmation
- Story ring: neon glow pulse on new stories
- Crowd meter: smooth colour transition (green → amber → red)
- Feed: smooth scroll, no jarring transitions
- All animations under 300ms

---

## Logo Usage

- Minimum size: 32px height
- Always on dark background
- Never stretch or distort
- Clear space: equal to the height of the letter "B" on all sides
- Acceptable: white logo on dark, neon purple logo on dark
- Never: logo on light background, logo with drop shadow

---

## Voice & Tone

- **Short and punchy** — this is a mobile app, not an essay
- **Nightlife energy** — casual, confident, fun
- **Never corporate** — no "utilise", no "leverage", no "synergy"
- **Inclusive** — welcoming to all 18+ non-Muslim users in KL
- Push notification tone: direct and exciting ("Marcus just checked in at Zouk 🔥")
- Error messages: human ("That didn't work — try again")
- Empty states: encouraging ("No posts yet — get out there tonight 🦉")

## Mascot — The Owl

The Barblink owl is perched on a spilled martini glass. It represents:
- Nocturnal (active at night, like our users)
- Wise (knows where to go)
- Watchful (sees everything happening in the KL nightlife scene)

Usage:
- Splash screen
- App icon
- Empty state illustrations
- Landing page hero
- Error screens
